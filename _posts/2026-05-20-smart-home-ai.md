Smart lights, AI thermostats, robot vacuums, voice-controlled everything. After 6 months of full home automation, here's the honest truth about what's worth it.

---

I went all in. Replaced every light switch, installed sensors in every room, connected every device to an AI hub. Six months later, I can tell you: most of it was unnecessary. But the parts that work? They genuinely changed how I live.

---

## The Setup

**Hub:** Home Assistant (open source, runs on a Raspberry Pi)
**AI Layer:** OpenClaw agent connected via MCP
**Voice:** Alexa + custom voice commands through local Whisper
**Network:** Zigbee mesh (30+ devices), Wi-Fi for cameras

**Total investment:** ~€800 over 6 months

---

## What Actually Improved My Life

### 1. AI-Powered Climate Control ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

The AI learns when I'm home, when I sleep, and adjusts heating/cooling automatically. It checks the weather forecast, my calendar, and even my sleep schedule.

**Result:** Energy bill dropped 22%. I haven't touched a thermostat in 4 months.

### 2. Smart Lighting with Presence Detection ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Lights turn on when I enter a room, turn off when I leave. No switches, no voice commands. It just works.

**The trick:** Don't use motion sensors alone — combine motion + door sensors + AI prediction.

### 3. Automated Morning Routine ⭐⭐⭐⭐

At my wake time: bedroom lights slowly brighten, coffee machine starts, morning news plays, thermostat adjusts, blinds open.

**Result:** Eliminates 15 minutes of morning decision-making.

### 4. Security Automation ⭐⭐⭐⭐

When I leave: all lights off, doors locked, cameras armed, robot vacuum starts. When I arrive: reverse.

**Result:** Peace of mind. I never wonder "did I lock the door?"

---

## What Was a Waste of Time

### 1. Voice Control for Everything ⭐⭐

**Reality:** "Sorry, I didn't understand that." × 10
**Better approach:** Presence detection and app control are faster and more reliable 95% of the time.

### 2. Smart Appliances ⭐⭐

**Smart fridge:** Tells me when food expires (I have eyes)
**Smart washing machine:** Sends a notification when done (I have ears)
**Verdict:** Most smart appliances add complexity without value.

### 3. Robot Vacuum as Daily Driver ⭐⭐⭐

**Reality:** Still needs bin emptying, hair untangling, furniture rescue. Good for daily light cleaning, but you still need a real vacuum weekly.

---

## The AI Integration: What Makes It Special

The difference between "smart home" and "AI home" is the learning layer.

**Traditional:** IF motion detected THEN turn on light
**AI:** Predicts when you'll enter the room, pre-adjusts lighting based on time and activity, learns your preferences

**Examples:**
- Predictive arrival: House starts warming 15 min before you usually get home
- Activity recognition: TV watching → dim lights. Desk work → bright task lighting
- Anomaly detection: Unusual motion at 3 AM → alert

---

## My Recommendation: Start Here

1. **Home Assistant** on Raspberry Pi (€80)
2. **Presence sensors** for main rooms (€30)
3. **Smart switches** (not bulbs!) for main lights (€60)

**Total: €170 for 80% of the benefit.**

Skip voice control. Skip smart appliances. Focus on presence-based automation.

The best smart home is one you never think about.
